---
term_id: panel
title: "Panel"
related_terms: step
---

Expandable content that appears over the viewer when you click "Learn more" or similar buttons. Panels provide additional context without leaving the current step.

Telar supports two layers of panels:
- **Layer 1**: Opens from the main step
- **Layer 2**: Opens from within layer 1

This progressive disclosure allows readers to choose their depth of engagement.
