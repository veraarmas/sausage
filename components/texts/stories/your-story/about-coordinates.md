This step zooms to **x=0.477, y=0.125, zoom=8.9**. The x and y values (0-1) position the view within the image. The zoom value magnifies the detail.

Natalie Cobo uses this technique to guide readers' attention to specific details that build her argument. Each coordinate choice serves the narrative - here, extreme zoom emphasizes Spain's symbolic position as the "head" of empire.

**In your Google Sheet:**
- Change x/y to pan to different parts of the image
- Increase zoom to see more detail
- Each step can show a different view of the same image
